Lucky Review

Locally owned chained, not owned by Kroger's, but by Dave's (the one in Cleveland). High prices, decent quality. I can find alot of bulk items, but not since COVID, and specialty herbs. I had a friend there once who worked in the meat department but he walked out on the job. You can walk around with a beer and/or wine while you're shopping so that's cool. Overall 3/5 stars.
